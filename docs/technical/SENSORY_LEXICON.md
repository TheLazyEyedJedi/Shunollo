## 1. Somatosensory (Touch & Texture)
**Bio Function**: Detects surface properties (Roughness), vibration (Meissner/Pacinian), and temperature.
**Cyber Equivalent**: The "Feel" of the Data Stream.
*   **Roughness (Texture)**: Information Density (Entropy) + Compression Ratio.
*   **Vibration (Flutter)**: Inter-arrival Jitter (Time Variance).
*   **Temperature (Volatility)**: Rate of Change (Flux) in volume/frequency.

## 2. Proprioception (Body Awareness)
**Bio Function**: Senses muscle stretch (Spindles) and tendon tension (Golgi Organs). "Where am I in space?"
**Cyber Equivalent**: The "Strain" on the System.
*   **Muscle Stretch**: CPU/RAM Utilization.
*   **Tension**: Queue Depth (Backpressure).
*   **Kinesthesia**: Throughput Velocity (Rate of Action).

## 3. Vestibular (Balance & Orientation)
**Bio Function**: Senses gravity, acceleration, and head position (Inner Ear). "Which way is down?"
**Cyber Equivalent**: The "Stability" of the Connection.
*   **Balance**: Routing Stability (Route Flapping).
*   **Acceleration**: Burstiness (Sudden spikes in traffic).
*   **Orientation**: Connection State (TCP State Machine).

## 4. Nociception (Pain & Damage)
**Bio Function**: Detects noxious stimuli that cause tissue damage. Warning system.
**Cyber Equivalent**: The "Integrity" of the Service.
*   **Mechanical Damage**: Dropped Packets / Retransmissions.
*   **Chemical Burn**: HTTP 5xx Errors / Exceptions.
*   **Thermal Stress**: Latency Spikes (Timeout proximity).

## 5. Olfaction (Smell / Distance Chemical)
**Bio Function**: Detects airborne chemicals (volatile) from a distance. Graph-based (Combinatorial).
**Cyber Equivalent**: The "Trace" of the Actor.
*   **Pungency**: Graph Centrality (How connected is this node?).
*   **Scent Trail**: Threat Intelligence Matches (Known bad actors).
*   **Pheromones**: Protocol Handshakes (Hellos).

## 6. Gustatory (Taste / Contact Chemical)
**Bio Function**: Detects chemicals on contact (ingestion). Sweet/Bitter/Sour.
**Cyber Equivalent**: The "Flavor" of the Payload.
*   **Sweetness**: Valid, structured JSON/XML (Easy to digest).
*   **Bitterness**: Malicious Payloads (Shellcode/Exploits).
*   **Sourness**: Malformed/Corrupt Data.
